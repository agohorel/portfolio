about

Hi, I'm Alex Gohorel, a freelance web developer and graphic designer based in Philadelphia. In many ways I am a very technical, detail-oriented person, but I am also a lifelong musician and visual artist, which I believe gives me a unique advantage in the field of web development. I ran a boutique record label for 8 years which is how I got my start in web development and where I picked up a lot of my branding and design chops. I quickly fell down the rabbit hole, and here we are! When I'm not working I can be found tinkering with personal projects at The Hive 76, producing music, or making generative artwork with p5.js.

I am well versed in HTML, CSS, and Javascript and I use a host of tools like postcss, SASS, NPM, and github to keep both my code and my development process performant. I mostly work with front-end technologies, but I have a fair amount of experience working with Node.js, Express, and Electron, and I'm always eager to learn and gain more experience on the back-end.
