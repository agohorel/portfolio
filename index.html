about

Hi, my name is Alex Gohorel. I'm a Philadelphia based web developer and designer.

I love making things.

As far back as I can remember I've been mesmerized by the creative potential of technology. Some of my earliest memories are doodling in MacPaint on my dad's black and white Macintosh 128k. A few years later I picked up Macromedia Flash and started designing graphics and making animations. Fast forward a few more years and I decided to start a record label for experimental electronic music which taught me a lot about branding and design and eventually exposed me to the world of "creative coding". In 2012 I started learning a programming language for creating multimedia applications called Processing and developed my own software for performing live, audio-reactive visuals. Around this time I realized that this brave new world of programming was perhaps the most powerful creative outlet for me - a way to synthesize all my creative experience into a single practice.

Since then, I have refocused my energy from working in the music industry to learning web development and continuing to hone my programming skills. I quit my comfortable music industry job of 7 years and took some time off to take online courses, build projects to put the theory to practice, and take on some freelance projects to get some real world experience. I am now seeking employment and/or freelance opportunities.

If you are seeking a customized web presence or are an employer looking to hire, please get in touch!

bend

Bend is a bespoke photobooth installation made for Anomalia Festival. It runs a custom application written in Processing (Java) which iteratively corrupts the images it takes, displaying them in sequence to the user as their image becomes progressively more corrupted.

  • Raspberry Pi

absolute racket

Absolute Racket is an audiovisual synthesizer you can play in a browser. Notes are played on the keyboard while the mouse tweaks parameters that allow for a surprisingly diverse selection of sounds. All sounds are visualized in realtime, giving additional feedback to the user.
